The next 4 exercises will help acquaint the reader with the concept of the sum of two subspaces. First, we'll need a definition.

Definisie 1.6.30

Let \(V\) be a vector space. Suppose \(U\) and \(W\) are two subspaces of \(V\text{.}\) The sum \(U + W\) of \(U\) and \(W\) is defined by

\begin{equation*} U + V = \{ \ve u + \ve w \in V \, : \, \ve u \in U, \ve w \in W \} \end{equation*}

In the exercises below, \(V,U,W\) will be as above.
