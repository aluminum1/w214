Stelling 2.2.10 Lineêre kombinase van voorafgaande vektore

Laat \(\ve{v}_1\text{,}\) \(\ve{v}_2\text{,}\) \(\ldots\text{,}\) \(\ve{v}_n\) 'n lys vektore in 'n vektorruimte \(V\) wees. Die volgende stellings is ekwivalent:

  1. Die lys vektore \(\ve{v}_1\text{,}\) \(\ve{v}_2\text{,}\) \(\ldots\text{,}\) \(\ve{v}_n\) is lineêr afhanklik.

  2. Óf \(\ve{v}_1 = \ve{0}\text{,}\) óf vir 'n \(r \in \{2, 3, \ldots, n\}\text{,}\) is \(\ve{v}_r\) 'n lineêre kombinasie van \(\ve{v}_1\text{,}\) \(\ve{v}_2\text{,}\) \(\ldots\text{,}\) \(\ve{v}_{r-1}\text{.}\)
