Stelling 2.5.7 Basisomskakeling

Veronderstel dat \(\basis{B} = \left\{ \ve{b}_1, \ldots, \ve{b}_n\right\}\) en \(\basis{C} = \left\{ \ve{c}_1, \ldots, \ve{c}_n \right\}\) basisse vir 'n vektorruimte \(V\) is, en laat \(\mat{P}_{\basis{C} \leftarrow \basis{B}}\) die basiskomskakelingsmatriks van \(\basis{B}\) na \(\basis{C}\) wees. Dan, vir alle vektore \(\ve{v}\) in \(V\text{,}\)

\begin{equation} [\ve{v}]_\basis{C} = \mat{P}_{\basis{C} \leftarrow \basis{B}} [\ve{v}]_\basis{B} \text{.}\tag{2.5.7} \end{equation}
