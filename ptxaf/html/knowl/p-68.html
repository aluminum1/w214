Paragraaf

Hierdie data moet aan die volgende reëls vir alle \(\ve{u}, \ve{v}, \ve{w}\) in \(V\) en vir alle reële getalle \(k\) en \(l\) voldoen:

R1.

\(\ve{v} + \ve{w} = \ve{w} + \ve{v}\)

R2.

\((\ve{u} + \ve{v}) + \ve{w} = \ve{u} + (\ve{v} + \ve{w})\)

R3a.

\(\ve{0} + \ve{v} = \ve{v}\)

R3b.

\(\ve{v} + \ve{0} = \ve{v}\)

R4.

\(k \cdot (\ve{v} + \ve{w}) = k \cdot \ve{v} + k \cdot \ve{w}\)

R5.

\((k + l) \cdot \ve{v} = k \cdot \ve{v} + l \cdot {\ve{v}}\)

R6.

\(k \cdot (l \cdot \ve{v}) = (kl) \cdot \ve{v}\)

R7.

\(1 \cdot \ve{v} = \ve{v}\)

R8.

\(0 \cdot \ve{v} = \ve{0}\)
