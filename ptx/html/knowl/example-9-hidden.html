We will write \(\Col_n\) for the vector space \(\Mat_{n,1}\) of \(n\)-dimensional column vectors,

\begin{equation*} \Col_n = \left\{ \left[ \begin{array}{c} x_1 \\ x_2 \\ \vdots \\ x_n \end{array} \right] : x_1, \ldots, x_n \in \mathbb{R} \right\} \end{equation*}

So, \(\Col_n\) ‘is’ just \(\mathbb{R}^n\text{,}\) but we make explicit the fact that the components of the vectors are arranged in a column.
