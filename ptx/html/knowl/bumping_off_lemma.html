Proposition 2.2.10 Bumping Off Proposition

Suppose \(\basis{L} = \bopen \ve{l}_1, \ve{l}_2, \ldots, \ve{l}_m \bclose\) is a linearly independent list of vectors in a vector space \(V\text{,}\) and that \(\basis{S} = \bopen \ve{s}_1, \ve{s}_2, \ldots, \ve{s}_n \bclose\) spans \(V\text{.}\) Then \(m \leq n\text{.}\)
