Definition 1.6.1

A subset \(U \subseteq V\) of a vector space \(V\) is called a subspace of \(V\) if:

  • For all \(\ve{u}, \ve{u}' \in U\text{,}\) \(\ve{u}+\ve{u}' \in U\)

  • \(\ve{0} \in U\)

  • For all scalars \(k\) and all vectors \(\ve{u} \in U\text{,}\) \(k \smul\ve{u} \in U\)
