By Corollary 2.3.22, we can extend \(\ve{v}_1, \ve{v}_2, \ldots, \ve{v}_n\) to a basis for \(V\text{.}\) But \(V\) has dimension \(n\text{,}\) so the basis must contain only \(n\) vectors by Theorem 2.3.2 (Invariance of Dimension). So we have not added any vectors at all! Hence our original list was already a basis.
