If you encounter a worked example, try to write out the example yourself. Perhaps the example is trying to show that \(A\) equals \(B\text{.}\) Start by asking yourself: Do I understand what ‘\(A\)’ actually means? Do I understand what ‘\(B\)’ actually means? Only then are you ready to consider the question of whether \(A\) is equal to \(B\) !
